Passion Fruit Cream

Description

Easy-to-make, fresh and delicious passion fruit cream that nobody can resist

Ingredients

  • 250g cream cheese
  • 2 cups double cream
  • 4 passion fruits

Steps

  1. Add the pulp of the passion fruits to a pan with a couple of tablespoons of water
  2. Cook on medium-low heat until the pulp has softened
  3. Pour the syrup through a strainer and mash with a spoon to separate the seeds from the pulp
  4. Carefully put the seeds into a small bowl
  5. In a large bowl, mix the cream cheese, the double cream and the pulp from the passion fruits until smooth
  6. Pour the mixture through a strainer
  7. Let rest overnight in the fridge
  8. Serve in cups, spreading a thin layer of seeds on top of the cream
